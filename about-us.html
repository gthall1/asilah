About Us

Our mission is to provide nothing but the best for our clients and their customers. We work our hardest to provide you with confidence in our services and expertise when it comes to tailoring your wardrobe. We aim to build trusting relationships with our clients and hope to be able to work with you for years to come. Let us show you what a great experience it is to work with us!

Our Story

Our owner was born in Casablanca, Morocco and began practicing his craft of tailoring at the age of 15. Chasing a passion for tailoring and visions of the American Dream he moved his family to the United States to continue his craft and work with some of the bigget names in fashion. His work speaks for itself and is highly sought after from years of working with brands like Valentino, Lugi Borrelli, and Kiton. While continuously inspired by the work that he's able to do every day he always remembers the difficulties and struggles he had to overcome to create a new life for his family and follow his dreams. Our shop is a culmination of those years of hard work and dreams, and we're proud to be able to offer you world-class tailoring services. It is our pleasure to serve you, and we look forward to building lasting relationships for years to come.

The Shop

Our shop name was inspired by the city of Asilah in Northern Morocco. Asilah is known for its creative culture and as a host for yearly music and art festivals. Artist's murals line the streets providing an ever-changing backdrop that serves as a window to the soul of the city. Fashion trends accompany the changing murals as the tailors of the city serve as artists with clothes being their medium of choice. Not just fashion but the fit and styling of the tailors of Asilah is a lifelong passion, and that is the inspiration and motivation that we seek to bring to our work every single day.
